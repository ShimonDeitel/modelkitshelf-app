Privacy Policy

Model Kit Shelf is an offline-first app. All data you enter (your kit collection) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, tracking, or third-party ad SDKs in this app.

The only network access this app makes is to Apple's App Store servers for in-app purchase (Pro upgrade) processing, which is handled entirely by StoreKit and Apple, not by us.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
